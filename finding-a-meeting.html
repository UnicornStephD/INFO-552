Al-Anon offers meetings in several formats. Each one is free to attend, anonymous, and welcoming to newcomers:

  • In-person meetings: Held in community centers, churches, and public spaces. Great for face-to-face connection.
  • Phone meetings: Join by calling in. Ideal for those without internet or who prefer not to use a screen.
  • Online meetings: Connect via Zoom or similar platforms. Convenient and accessible from anywhere.
  • Mobile app meetings: Use the Al-Anon Family Groups app to find meetings on the go.
