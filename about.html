About Me

Hi, I’m Stephanie Desjardins — a librarian with a deep love for history, books, and culture. With over 15 years of experience in information and data management, I find joy in organizing complex records and preserving the past to make it accessible for the future. My passion for historical research and genealogy helps me gain a deeper understanding of life, identity, and connection — both personal and collective.

I’m currently pursuing my Master’s in Library and Information Science at Drexel University, a path that aligns beautifully with my love for information, culture, and the preservation of history. Outside of work, I enjoy reading, baking, visiting museums, attending symphonies and plays, listening to music of all kinds, and spending time with my husband Garret, our dog Talula, and our loved ones. I also find inspiration in travel and creative projects, including photography and crafting.

Originally from Western Massachusetts and Southern Maine, I’ve called Philadelphia home for the past 15 years. This fall, we’re relocating to New England, and I’m excited to see where this next chapter leads.
